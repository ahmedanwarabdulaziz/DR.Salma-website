## ðŸŒ¿ Hormone-Supporting Foods

### Phytoestrogens

**What They Are:**
Natural plant compounds that can help balance hormones by mimicking estrogen in the body.

**Best Sources:**
- **Soy Products**: Tofu, tempeh, edamame, miso
- **Seeds**: Flaxseeds, sesame seeds, pumpkin seeds
- **Legumes**: Chickpeas, lentils, black beans
- **Fruits**: Apples, pears, berries

### Cruciferous Vegetables

**Why They Matter:**
- Support liver detoxification and hormone metabolism
- Help balance estrogen levels naturally
- Rich in antioxidants and anti-inflammatory compounds
- Support thyroid function

**Best Sources:**
- Broccoli and broccoli sprouts
- Brussels sprouts
- Kale and collard greens
- Cabbage and cauliflower
- Bok choy and arugula

## âš ï¸ Foods to Limit

### Processed Foods
- **Why Avoid**: High in refined sugars, artificial ingredients, and unhealthy fats
- **Impact**: Can disrupt hormonal balance and cause inflammation
- **Alternatives**: Choose whole, unprocessed foods

### Excessive Caffeine
- **Why Limit**: Can affect sleep quality and stress hormones
- **Impact**: May worsen PMS symptoms and anxiety
- **Alternatives**: Herbal teas, decaf coffee, water

### Alcohol
- **Why Moderate**: Can affect liver function and hormone metabolism
- **Impact**: May disrupt sleep and hormonal balance
- **Guideline**: 1 drink per day maximum for women
